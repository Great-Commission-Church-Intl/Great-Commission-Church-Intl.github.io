GCCI EM Video & Broadcast System User Manual

The English Sanctuary's video and broadcast system consists of 8 major components that work together to combine 3 inputs from two PCs and a camcorder and broadcasts those inputs to 2 projectors, the 3 TVs in the second floor hallway, and YouTube or Zoom as well. The system is able to produce an output with a resolution up to 1080p @ 30fps or 60fps.

You can mouse over each component below to see how they are connected, or you can click on a component and see details about the component as well as where it is located in the sound booth.
