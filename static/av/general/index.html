GCCI EM A/V System General Information

This is a comprehensive list of general information and important reminders concerning operation of the system.

1. The speaker amplifiers and the subwoofer may NOT be turned on before the digital mixing console.

2. The admin login for the digital mixing console is reserved for English Ministry Staff and Grace Harvest Staff. All other users, please use the guest login.

3. Do NOT increased the level of the main volume slider past digital 0. The system is configured to be loud enough below that level.

4. Please do not adjust the frequencies of the microphones or the in-ear monitors.

1. At times, if the camcorder is plugged into the system after the ATEM switcher is already on, the camera ouput will not be recognized. To remedy this, power cycle Sequence D on the power sequencer.
